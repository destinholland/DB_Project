Query 5 calculates the number of days that are hotter than 'x' standard deviation(s) less than the summer's average heat index value.

First the summer's average heat index value is calculated for the months of May to September. Then, the standard deviation is calculated for the summer months as well.

We then subtract the standard deviation value times the user's input from the summer average heat index and count the number of days hotter than that value.
